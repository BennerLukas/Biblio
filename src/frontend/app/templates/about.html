Biblio is build by a group of students.

It's made for your private book collection and library. So you can find old gems, borrow books to friends or take note of all your read and unread books more easily.

It's using Python with Flask as a backend. The database is handled in form of a postgresql and is connected with the python backend via psycopg2.
